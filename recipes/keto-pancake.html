Keto Pancake

Make extra thick and fluffy pancakes with just 5 ingredients! No grains and no sugar needed, they are quick and easy to make! 3 grams net carbs per serving.

You need: Oatmeal, eggs, honey, lemon zest and coconut oil

There’s nothing more comforting than a big stack of pancakes for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups of oatmeal
  • 1 spoon of honey
  • Coconut oil
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together oatmeal, honey, eggs, and lemon zest until smooth.
  2. In a nonstick skillet over medium-low heat, melt 1 tablespoon of coconut oil. Pour in about 3 tablespoons batter and cook until golden, 2 minutes. Flip and cook 2 minutes more. Transfer to a plate and repeat with remaining batter.
  3. Serve with preferred topings.
